# Submission Outline

## Short Pitch

REWIND RUNNER is a loop puzzle runner where every recorded past self becomes a live ghost that can hold switches, block lasers, carry Small or Heavy states, and sync the final escape route.

## Why It Fits A Mini-Game Challenge

```text
Immediate controls: move, dash, record.
Player-choice loop: the player decides exactly when a route becomes a ghost.
Clear novelty: past selves and body-state items are tools, not just replays.
Short session: twelve compact rooms.
Repeat appeal: faster clears, fewer ghosts, and better star records.
```

## Demo Flow

```text
1. Main intro slides.
2. Room 1: record G1 on switch.
3. Room 2: ghost blocks laser.
4. Room 3: timing gate plus Small item.
5. Room 4: two-loop sync plus Heavy switch.
6. Room 5: three role-specific ghosts open the first escape gate.
7. Room 6-8: combined scale, laser, and first four-ghost sync.
8. Room 9-12: boost chain, split Small/Heavy lanes, dual laser block, final loop break.
9. Result screen: LOOP BROKEN.
```

## Required Submission Notes

```text
No backend.
No login.
No real-time network features.
No paid content.
Runs directly in a browser.
```
